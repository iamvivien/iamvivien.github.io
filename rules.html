Objectives

CYCLEMYLYFE is a VR-based application. This will be designed as close as possible to the streets and park connectors in the Real Environment (RE).

To improve safety from cycling in Singapore

Allow cyclists and Personal Mobility Devices (PMD) users to practice safe riding in Singapore

To promote empathy between cyclists and non-cyclists

To provide a feedback component for interaction between users and the developers of CYCLEMYLYFE.
